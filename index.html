Discord Verifier by tfpk

This program allows you to verify UNSW students and add them to your discord server, automatically!

How to Signup? Follow the instructions on this form!

Features for Students

  • Instant Verification - enter your zID/zPass, discord username, and agree to the terms; and you're in!

Features for Admins

  • List all zIDs of server members in one place!
  • Configure the messages your members see!
